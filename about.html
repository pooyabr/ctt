Our Story

Cơm Tấm THANH (Pronounced Kom Tham Tainh) is a family-owned business that was established by a husband and a wife back in 1998, nearly 20 years ago. Since then we have expanded to a total of three locations within the Bay Area. However, business was not always booming as it is now; the business had a rough start at first. In the beginning, there was only a few workers, including the owners, and at times there were only two people working. The restaurant sometimes had to even open until 4 AM in the morning to make ends meet in the past. In an area where there is a lot of competition, our customers choose to come and eat out our restaurant. Thus, we are truly grateful to our customers for their continuous support. Without our beloved customers, we would not be where we are today.

We strive to provide quality authentic Vietnamese cuisine and with our extensive menu, we hope to be able to offer dishes of many varieties to suit everyone's tastes. In addition to the popular Vietnamese beef soup, we also serve Cơm Tấm or steamed broken rice with various types of grilled pork or beef and Nước Chấm or sweet and sour dipping sauce. Unlike other Vietnamese restaurants, we also offer other unique delicious dishes, like Bún Bò Huế, Bánh Cuốn and Caramel River Bard.
